Areas of work

Kinesiology

The science of human movement aimed at improving health. We deal with kinesiology of the spine, lower extremities and muscle imbalances that disrupt the integrity of the body.

Strength Training

Focused on building muscle strength and endurance through personalized workout plans. We help you achieve physical fitness and structural balance.

Posture Correction

Improve your posture and alleviate discomfort through targeted exercises that correct imbalances, realign the body, and enhance physical function.

Weight Management

Personalized plans for achieving and maintaining a healthy weight. We provide guidance on nutrition, exercise, and lifestyle changes for lasting results.

Breathing and Relaxation Techniques

Learn techniques to reduce stress, improve breathing patterns, and enhance relaxation. This practice promotes mental clarity and physical calmness.

Lifestyle and Habit Coaching

Focused on helping you develop positive habits and sustainable lifestyle changes. We work together to improve productivity, well-being, and life satisfaction.

Sports Performance Coaching

Designed to enhance athletic performance. We create sport-specific training plans to improve speed, strength, agility, and endurance.

Recovery and Rehabilitation

Specialized recovery programs for injury rehabilitation and physical therapy. We help you regain strength, flexibility, and overall function.

Mental Health Coaching

A holistic approach to mental wellness. We help individuals manage stress, anxiety, and promote emotional balance and clarity.

Flexibility Training

Improve your range of motion and mobility through customized flexibility programs. Our approach enhances movement and prevents injury.

Cardio Fitness Training

Focus on improving heart health and endurance through personalized cardiovascular exercises. Our goal is to enhance your stamina and overall fitness.

Neuromuscular Re-education

Techniques aimed at restoring movement patterns and muscle function after injury or dysfunction. We focus on improving coordination between muscles and nerves for optimal performance.

Gait Analysis and Correction

Comprehensive evaluation of walking patterns to identify and correct abnormalities. Our programs are designed to enhance mobility and reduce pain associated with improper gait.

Vestibular Rehabilitation

Focused on treating balance disorders and dizziness through specialized exercises. We help retrain the brain and body to improve stability and spatial orientation.

Functional Movement Screening

Assessment of fundamental movement patterns to detect limitations or asymmetries. Our tailored programs address these issues to enhance functional performance and reduce injury risk.

Chronic Pain Management

Specialized strategies to alleviate and manage long-term pain conditions. Our approach focuses on restoring quality of life through targeted movement therapies and pain relief techniques.
